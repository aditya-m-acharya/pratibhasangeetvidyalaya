Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya

Founder: Shri. Vasantrao Vishnupurikar

Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya is an educational and cultural organization based in Mulund, Mumbai (India). The school was founded by Shri. Vasantrao Vishnupurikar in 1977. Since then it is committed to serving students and talented musicians, from around the world by providing opportunities to learn, perform, teach and share their talents.

This institute teaches "Tabla" as the main instrument. Harmonium classes are also conducted in this institute. With the blessings of the gurus and the music lovers like you the institute has completed its 43 years successfully and continues to grow.

Gurus at Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya

Shri Vasantrao Vishnupurikar

Founder of Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya

Shri Vasantrao Vishnupurikar is a great tabla player and even better a guru. He got the opportunity to learn from a lot of great traditional tabla maestros of the country and then pass on the traditional compositions as it is to the next generation. His style consists of bold and clear execution of syllables, precision in rhythm, and colossal repertoire of compositions and improvisational techniques making his concerts a sheer joy!

Pandit Pravin Deshpande

Harmonium Teacher at Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya

Pandit Pravin Deshpande is an excellent harmonium player. He is a disciple of Pandit Anant Kemkarji. He has not only excelled in playing harmonium but also has received tabla guidance from Pandit ........ ...... for 9 years. He is top B grade artist at All India Radio. He also teaches harmonium at Vallabh Sangeet Vidyalaya, Sion. His command over laya and his fast moving fingers over the keys can make listening to him a great pleasure.

Shri Makarand Vishnupurikar

Co-Tabla Teacher at Pratibha Sangeet Vidyalaya

Shri Makarand Vishnupurikar is the son of guru Shri Vasantrao Vishupurikar. Since tabla was already a part of his home and life since childhood, he began learning tabla at age of 4 from his father. Later, he received training from a great tabla maestro Pandit Suresh (Bhai) Gaitonde. He also was a rank holder in visharad exam conducted by Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya throughout the country. Today he is a great accompanist to semi-classical form of music. He didn't just stop at excelling tabla but also plays all the percussion instruments as an accompanist.

Late Pt. Annasaheb Thatte

Read more

Late Pt. Annasaheb Thatte was a gandabandh disciple of one of the greatest tabla players and composers of all time "Ustaad Amir Hussain Khan". When Vasantrao Vishnupurikarji returned from Hyderabad, he met Pt. Annasaheb Thatte and started training under him.

Late Pt. Nandakumar (Hyderabad)

Read more

Late Pt. Nandakumar (Hyderabad) was one of the seniormost disciples of Ustad Shaik Dawood Khan. He learnt from the great maestro for more than 40 years. He had a special afffection with Vasantrao Vishnupurikar and then taught him after the sad demise of Ustad Shaikh Dawood Khan.
